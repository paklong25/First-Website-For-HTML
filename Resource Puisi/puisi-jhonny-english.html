Title: "Flawless Agent, Failed Without Hesitation"

Behind a neat suit and a twisted tie,
An agent stands, without a bit of fear,
Johnny English, a name that is spoken proudly,
Although in every mission, failure greets.

His steps are firm, full of confidence,
But often, the direction is not in line,
The sophisticated gadget in his hand is not always right,
But somehow, he always survives.

The world is in danger, the enemy is waiting,
But Johnny comes, with an innocent style,
Destiny laughs at his actions,
Because success comes through a series of mistakes.

A big explosion, then falls without a plan,
He rises again, as if it were all just a play,
With a sly smile and a burning spirit,
He never gives up, even though he is chased by disaster.

There is nothing more awkward than his movements,
But in the midst of mistakes, he achieves victory,
Johnny English, an unpredictable agent,
In his silliness, the world is still safe too.

Behind the jokes and blunders that decorate,
Hidden is an intention that is always firm,
Because even though it is not perfect, it still survives,
The agent is invincible, with laughter as a friend.
