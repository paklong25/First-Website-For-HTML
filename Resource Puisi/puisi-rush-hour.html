Title: "Two Worlds, One Goal"

In the bustling city alleys,
Two souls meet in one movement,
One from the east, full of tranquility,
One from the west, jokes without limits.

The agile Lee, moves like the wind,
Silently present without a cold sound,
The loud Carter, brings cheerful laughter,
His tongue is sharp, always brave to be clear.

They are different, the world is not the same,
But the enemy in front of them remains the same,
In pursuit, in laughter and running,
They are united, never stopping.

Footsteps dancing in the fight,
Laughter in the gap, destroying disaster,
Among the bullets, behind the secret,
They win, with extraordinary style.

Two worlds, one definite goal,
Guarding what is right, defending without stopping,
In Rush Hour, they conquer the world,
In action and laughter, forever making a sound.
